Shalimar productions History

Shalimar Production Company is engaged in the business of Media and Media segments of Motion Picture, Video, and TV Programs. The company is engaged in the production of different types of regional albums and films, the company has also developed various media albums and short films in Rajasthani language.




The company’s studios have various locations, such as buildings, parks, rivers, streets, markets, villages, temples, police stations, open fields, restaurants and hotels, and libraries. The company’s Shalimar Academy, located at Shalimar House, Bikaner, trains, develops and molds artists through its trainers and mentors.
